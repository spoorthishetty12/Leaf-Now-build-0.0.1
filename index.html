Some common questions
were often asked

Which is better-planters or planting in the soil?

Growing in planter or in soil is dependent on space,time and resources available at ones disposal. But Bonsai can grow only in planter and trees in ground.

Which are the best indoor plants?

Indoor plants do not need too much sun, thus can stay in shade or semi shade areas. Easy to msintsin are Arica Palm, Chlorophtym,money plant etc. They also are great purifiers.

What causes brown crispy leaves?

Browning leaves are typically caused by under watering sunburn, or overwatering. If the tips are turning brown and crunchy, the soil likely became too dry for too long in between waterings. This can also cause the plant to drop leaves.

Which plants work best in pots?

Plants with shallow roots works best in pots.

How often should we replace soils in pots?

Pots in soil should be replaced once in a year either in February or during the rainy season.

How often do you recommend fertilizing?

If the plant is potted in good soil mix, fertilizing can be done twice a year for perennial plants.
